About us

MediFlow is an AI-driven hospital management app designed to streamline healthcare operations. Powered by Clara, our intelligent AI assistant, the app optimizes patient triage, scheduling, ambulance routing, thermal screening, and staff workload management.

By integrating real-time data and predictive analytics, MediFlow enhances efficiency, reduces waiting times, and ensures better patient care, making hospitals smarter and more responsive.
